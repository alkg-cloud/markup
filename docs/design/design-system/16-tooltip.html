16 — Tooltip

The single tooltip primitive (src/components/Tooltip/TooltipPortal.tsx). A <div popover="hint" id="markup-tooltip"> rendered once at root layout; a capture-phase document listener watches mouseenter / focusin on every [data-tooltip] trigger, copies the text, positions against the trigger's getBoundingClientRect, and calls showPopover(). Top-layer paint escapes every overflow ancestor and stacking context. ~150 ms dwell before show; instant hide on leave/scroll.
