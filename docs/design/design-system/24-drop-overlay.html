24 — Drop Overlay

Full-bleed overlay that appears the moment a file enters the window during drag-n-drop upload. Sits at z-index: 90 above sidebar, topbar, content, and any mockup iframe. Dismisses on dragleave (120 ms fade) or routes to the New Mockup dialog on drop. Four variants cover the range from imposing scrim to discreet chip.
